Simple fun number and ball popping game

Drop The Number Seven is a ball popping game based on merging balls and numbers.

Carefully drop the top ball down according to its number. After balls merge with the ball horizontally or vertically; numbered balls that are equal to the number of balls merged popped. If the popping ball triggers a new explosion; a combo occurs.

For get a high-score point, drop the ball and merge it with others carefully, match the number then pop the balls with combos.

The game automatically saves your progress to your device, then you can continue from where you left off.

Players who like ad-free games can play the game offline by turning off the internet. However; Coins cannot be earned because no advertisements are watched in use offline. The Drop Seven is a free app but ad-based. The choice is up to you.

In your free time; play this game, drop the ball and match the numbers, merge it with other balls, pop them with combos, and relax your mind by having fun.
