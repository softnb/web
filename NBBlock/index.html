NB Block Puzzle is a Tetris like, classic cube block game, on the English language. It designed for mobile phone and tablets.

Drag and drop blocks into empty areas. Reserve the spaces for the next block, explode the rows and columns together. A planned placement gives the possibility of 6 combos. So you get high score.

Also some of the cube blocks can be rotatable. If you have the coins, you can rotate or pull the cube blocks to down with the rotate and magnet buttons. So you can easily place them into the gaps. You can get coins by making 3x combos or watching ads.

The application automatically saves your progress to your device with the save game feature and you can continue from where you left off later.

The players who want ad-free games can play the game without internet by turning off the internet. But do not forget; Rotate and magnet coins cannot be earned because no ads are watched in offline use. The app is free but ad-based. The choice is up to you.

In your spare time, play NB Block Puzzle, drop the cubes into the appropriate placement area and explode them. Relax your mind by having fun.
