Addictive Tetris-like, classic block puzzle games.

Block game: Drag and drop the blocks into the empty area, merge them on the rows and columns. Explode filled rows and columns and get points.

Slide Block: Slide the blocks and merge them on the rows then explode filled rows.

This block puzzle game provides an enjoyable gaming experience. It improves your logical skills and trains your brain.
